# ADR-0000: Architecture Decision Record Template

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded

## Context

What is the issue that we're seeing that is motivating this decision or change?

Describe the forces at play, including:
- Technical constraints and requirements
- Business requirements and priorities
- Team skills and resources
- Time constraints
- Other relevant factors

## Decision

What is the change that we're proposing and/or doing?

Clearly state the architectural decision and the rationale behind it.

Include:
- The chosen solution or approach
- Key components or technologies involved
- How this decision addresses the context described above

## Consequences

What becomes easier or more difficult to do because of this change?

### Positive Consequences
- List the benefits and improvements resulting from this decision
- Include improvements to system qualities (performance, scalability, maintainability, etc.)

### Negative Consequences
- List the trade-offs and limitations introduced by this decision
- Include technical debt, increased complexity, or reduced flexibility in certain areas

### Neutral Consequences
- List any notable changes that are neither clearly positive nor negative

- Link to related ADRs that provide context or are affected by this decision

### Examples
- Provide concrete examples or use cases that illustrate this decision

### References
